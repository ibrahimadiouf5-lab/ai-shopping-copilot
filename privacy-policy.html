Privacy Policy

AI Shopping Copilot · Last updated: July 2026
Summary: AI Shopping Copilot does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. We do not track you. We do not know who you are.

1. What we collect

AI Shopping Copilot collects no personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your browsing history or the websites you visit
  • Your location, device ID, or IP address
  • Any payment or financial information
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

2. How the extension works

When you open the extension on a supported shopping page, it reads the visible text content of that product page — the same text you can already see — and sends it to our secure backend server to generate an AI analysis. This content is:

  • Used only to generate the analysis for that specific request
  • Not stored on our servers after the response is returned
  • Not linked to any user identity
  • Discarded immediately after processing

3. Third-party services

The AI analysis is powered by Anthropic Claude. Product page content is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests. We do not share any additional user data with Anthropic beyond the product page text.

4. Data storage

No user data is stored on our servers. The extension may store your most recent analysis result temporarily in your browser's local storage so the popup loads faster on repeat visits. This data never leaves your device.

5. Permissions explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab / scripting: Required to read the product page text so the AI can analyse it. We only read the page you are currently viewing, and only when the extension popup is open.
  • storage: Used to temporarily cache the last analysis result in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
  • tabs: Used to detect which website you are on so we can show the correct analysis.

6. Children's privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@aishoppingcopilot.com
